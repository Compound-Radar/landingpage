Privacy notice.

Last updated: April 18, 2026

This notice explains how Compound Radar handles information collected through compoundradar.com and the inquiry form on this site.

Compound Radar is in pre-launch product development. This notice covers the landing page only. It does not cover a production SaaS product — that will have its own privacy policy and, where applicable, a separate business-associate agreement at launch.

What we collect

When you submit the inquiry form, we collect the information you give us directly:

  • Name
  • Work email
  • Pharmacy or company name
  • Role (optional)
  • Any message you include

We do not use website analytics, advertising pixels, or third-party trackers. No cookies are set by this site.

Why we collect it

To contact you about the design-partner pilot and to understand the peptide compliance needs of the pharmacies we're building for. That's it — no marketing lists, no onward selling.

How it's processed

Form submissions are handled by Formspree (our form processor) and delivered to our email inbox. A copy of each submission is retained in Formspree and in our email records.

How long we keep it

As long as is reasonable for our early-customer discovery work. You can request deletion at any time — see below.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your information, and we do not share it with third parties for marketing. The only third parties that touch your submission are:

  • Formspree — form processing
  • Our email provider — inbox storage

Your rights

You can ask us to:

  • Show you what we have on file for you
  • Correct or update it
  • Delete it

Email theresa@compoundradar.com with the subject line "Privacy request" and we'll respond within a reasonable period.

Changes to this notice

If we update this notice, the "Last updated" date above will change and the latest version will always be at compoundradar.com/privacy.html.

Contact

Questions about this notice or about Compound Radar generally — theresa@compoundradar.com.
